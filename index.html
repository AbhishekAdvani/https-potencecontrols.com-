POTENCE CONTROLS

Potence Controls Private Limited ( PCPL ) is a premier Manufacturer of Water Quality Analysers.
Along with manufacturing we also provide solutions for Real Time Monitoring of various Analytical parameters as per the needs of the application in Water, Gas & Process measurement.
Right from Design and Development to Commissioning, our analyser systems include but are not restricted to Panels / Purged Panels, Flame Proof and Ex-Proof enclosures, Sample Handling Systems etc.. Having worked on several Projects in India and Overseas with leading EPCs, Consultants and Licensors, we have ample expertise to serve this domain.
